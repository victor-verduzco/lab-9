The Space Needle

The Space Needle is one Seattle most popular attractions for locals and tourists alike! I personally got to see the Space Needle upclose and it was quite the sight to see. Make sure to plan your visit and buy tickets ahead of time as it fairly packed quickly for the day and more so if it's around holiday season.

Amazing Restaraunts

If you are craving seafood then you've come to the right place! Seattle has some of best and freshest seafood the United States has to offer! Located by the coast of the Atlantic Ocean, it's a hub for amazing seafood.

Coffee Addicts' Heaven

For the coffee nerds out there don't think we forgot about you! Seattle is home to some of the finest coffee known to man. The original starbucks store and roastery is located in the city and one of Seattle's most popular tourist visits. Make to sure to include it into your itenary for a taste of the homebrew coffee!!

Seattle Washington

Seattle, Washington is a large and properous city full of culture and history. From it's many culinary delights to the national landmarks, it's never a boring day in Seattle! Also known for it's rainy weather, Seatle will make for an unforgetabble visit for anyone who chooses to make it add it as a stop on their map.
